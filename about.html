The Project

Nikkoshi Pearls was born as an academic project to create a modern, accessible website showcasing the UNESCO-listed temples and shrines of Nikko, Japan. Built with pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, the site aims to capture the spiritual beauty and historical richness of one of Japan's most treasured heritage sites.

Our goal is to provide visitors with an immersive digital experience that inspires real-world exploration of these extraordinary sacred places.
