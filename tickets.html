Ticket Information

Please note that we do not sell tickets directly. We provide this information to help you plan your journey. For actual purchases and up-to-date pricing, please visit the official Tobu Railway ticketing website. Below are the most popular travel passes available.

Individual Shrines

¥300 - ¥1,300 / varies
  • Tosho-gu Shrine (¥1,300)
  • Rinno-ji Temple (¥400)
  • Futarasan Shrine (¥300)
  • Purchased separately at entrances
Official Site

Nikko Pass (World Heritage)

¥2,120 / adult
  • Round-trip from Asakusa (Tokyo)
  • Unlimited buses in World Heritage area
  • Valid for 2 consecutive days
  • Discounts at designated shops
  • *Shrine entry fees not included*
Official Site

Nikko Pass (All Area)

¥4,780 / adult
  • Round-trip from Asakusa (Tokyo)
  • Unlimited buses to Okunikko area
  • Access to Lake Chuzenji area
  • Valid for 4 consecutive days
  • *Shrine entry fees not included*
Official Site

🚆 Travel Tip

Limited express trains (like the Spacia X) require a separate limited express ticket in addition to the Nikko Pass. Make sure to reserve your seats in advance on the official Tobu website.

Visitor Information

Opening Hours

April – October: 8:00 – 17:00
November – March: 8:00 – 16:00

Getting There

2 hours from Tokyo via Tobu Railway or JR Nikko Line to Nikko Station.

Accessibility

Wheelchair-accessible paths available for main viewing areas. Guides on request.
